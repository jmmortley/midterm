Why Should You Get a Passport?

You should get a passport because there are so many beautiful places to visit outside of your hometown, state, and country.

Countries like France, Spain and Guatemala have such a rich and unique culture to offer and those that visit could benefit greatly.

What Else is a Passport Useful For?

Passports can act as a second form of identifcation. In case of emergency or civil unrest, passports can facilitate evacuation .
